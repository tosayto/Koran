

Chapter 84

The Splitting
al-Inshiqāq ( الإنشقاق )

25 verses • revealed at Meccan


»The surah that opens with a riveting description of The Splitting of the sky and the levelling of the earth as a sign of the commencement of the Day of Resurrection. It is named after the “splitting open” (inshiqāq) of the sky mentioned in verse 1. The surah deals with the inevitability of man’s meeting with his Lord on the Day of Judgement. The obedience of the sky and earth is contrasted with the disobedience of the disbelievers. The reaction of the believers and of the disbelievers on the Day of Judgement is described.« 

The surah is also known as: Bursting Asunder, Ripped Apart, Splitting Open, The Bursting, The Cleaving, The Rending, The Rending Asunder.

A. J. Arberry


Nay, but the unbelievers are crying lies,


Muhammad Asad


Nay, but they who are bent on denying the truth give the lie [to this divine writ]!


J. M. Rodwell


Yea, the unbelievers treat it as a lie.

Nay, those who misbelieve do say it is a lie,

23



وَاللَّهُ أَعلَمُ بِما يوعونَ



Fred Leemhuis


Maar God weet het best wat zij in zich bergen.


Salomon Keyzer


Maar God kent de kwaadwilligheid wel, die zij in hunne borsten verborgen houden.


Sofjan Siregar


Maar Allah weet het beste wat zij verbergen.


Edip Yüksel


ALLAH onların sakladıklarını çok iyi bilir.


Yasar Nuri Ozturk


Allah, içlerinde sakladıklarını çok iyi biliyor.






وَاللَّهُ أَعلَمُ بِما يوعونَ



A. J. Arberry


and God knows very well what they are secreting.


Muhammad Asad


Yet God has full knowledge of what they conceal [in their hearts].1


1.Namely, their unwillingness to admit their responsibility to a Supreme Being.


J. M. Rodwell


But God knoweth their secret hatreds:


E. H. Palmer


but God knows best the (malice) that they hide.


George Sale


But God well knoweth the malice which they keep hidden in their breasts.

Verkondig hun dan een pijnlijke bestraffing.


Salomon Keyzer


Kondig hun dus eene vreeselijke straf aan.


Sofjan Siregar


Verkondig hun dan een pijnlijke bestraffing.


Edip Yüksel


Onları acı bir azapla müjdele.


Yasar Nuri Ozturk


O halde, onlara acıklı bir azap muştula!






فَبَشِّرهُم بِعَذابٍ أَليمٍ



A. J. Arberry


So give them good tidings of a painful chastisement,


Muhammad Asad


Hence, give them the tiding of grievous suffering [in the life to come]


J. M. Rodwell


Let their only tidings be those of painful punishment;


E. H. Palmer


So give them the glad tidings of grievous woe!


George Sale


Wherefore denounce unto them a grievous punishment,
